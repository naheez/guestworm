About Maldives

Maldives is my beloved country of birth and I am living all my life in Maldives. The location of Maldives is west of Sri Lanka and south of India in the Indian Ocean close to the Arabian sea. It's an island archipelago consisting of coral islands distributed to nineteen atolls. The main income generating industries are tourism and fisheries. The language of all inhabitants is Dhivehi and the state religion is only Islam. The crime rate is relatively low. The current governing system is a presidential democratic system. The policial situation is getting stable at the time of publishing this website.
